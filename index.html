List of steps to make a sandwich.

  1. Grab all the essential items: 2 pieces of slide bread, 2 pieces of slide cheese, Mayonnaise and mustard. couole slide meat, some lettuce and slide tomatoes
  2. Grab 2 pieces of slide bread.
  3. Put couple layers of mayonnaise and mustard to one side of each bread.
  4. Lay one piece or more of the slide cheese on each side of the bread cover with layer mayonnaise and mustard.
  5. Lay one piece or more of slide meat on each side on the slide cheese.
  6. Lay some lettuces and tomatoes to one of the covered bread.
  7. Gently raise the covered bread with no lettuces or tomatoes and flip the bread to have the covered side to the other covered side.
